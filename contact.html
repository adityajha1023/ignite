Frequently asked questions about IGNITE Techfest 2025

How do I register for IGNITE Techfest?

Fill out the registration form above or visit our official website at iilmignite.com. You can also contact our student coordinators for assistance.

What types of events are available at IGNITE?

IGNITE features 35+ events including hackathons, coding contests, robo wars, drone races, model exhibitions, tech quizzes, and innovation challenges across Tech, Innovation, and Robotics.

Can students from other colleges participate?

Absolutely! IGNITE welcomes participants from all universities and institutions. The fest is open to students, innovators, and tech enthusiasts from across India.

What is the total prize pool for IGNITE 2025?

The total prize pool for IGNITE Techfest 2025 is worth 71.75 Lakhs+ with exciting prizes across all event categories and competitions.

When and where is IGNITE Techfest 2025?

IGNITE Techfest 2025 will be held on 15th & 16th April 2025 at IILM University, Knowledge Park II, Greater Noida. Mark your calendars!
