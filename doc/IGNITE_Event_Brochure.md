## About IILM University

IILM was established in 1993, under the aegis of the Ram Krishan & Sons Charitable Trust. With more than 30+ years of experience in training future entrepreneurs and managers, it has emerged as one of the distinguished Universities in the Delhi NCR area. 

The University has been set up under the Uttar Pradesh Private University (Amendment) Act 2022 with a vision to be **Global, Inclusive and Responsible** with focus on Engineering and Technology. It is located in 16-18 Knowledge Park II, Greater Noida, with a built area of over 26 acres known for its state-of-the-art facilities.

It is centrally located with safe and secure campuses in New Delhi, Gurgaon, and Greater Noida. At IILM University, a diverse range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs are offered across various disciplines. The university boasts a distinguished faculty body dedicated to fostering intellectual growth, research endeavours, and industry relevant education.

Beyond academics, students at IILM University experience a vibrant campus life enriched by various clubs, societies, and cultural events, promoting holistic development and fostering a sense of community. Additionally, the university's placement and career services play a pivotal role in preparing students for the professional world, offering internships, career counselling, and facilitating recruitment drives.

## About IGNITE Techfest

The IGNITE Techfest is a premier annual event that seamlessly integrates **technology, innovation, and collaboration**. 

**Theme:** *"Innovative Technologies for a Sustainable Future"*

This year's festival aims to inspire creativity and drive technological advancements that promote sustainability. Attendees will have the opportunity to:
- Showcase groundbreaking projects
- Engage in insightful discussions
- Pitch entrepreneurial ventures
- Present cutting-edge research that advances sustainability through technological innovation

IGNITE Techfest provides a dynamic platform for individuals from diverse fields to come together, celebrate technological breakthroughs, and foster a culture of innovation with a focus on sustainability.
