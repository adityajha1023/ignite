[ About IILM University ]

IILM was established in 1993 under the aegis of the Ram Krishan & Sons Charitable Trust. The University offers diverse undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across Engineering, Management, Law, Design, and Liberal Arts with state-of-the-art facilities spread over 26 acres.
