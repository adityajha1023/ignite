Robotics Event

Robo Race

"More speed, More power with minimum time"

Registration Fee / ₹700

15th & 16th April 2025

Registration Fee

₹700

Event Category

Robotics

Total Rounds

03

Design a manually controlled ROBOT that can cover maximum distance in shortest possible time, challenging hurdles and defeating opponents. Showcase your engineering skills and compete against the best robotics enthusiasts from across the country.

Competition Rounds

[ 3 Rounds ]

01

Elimination Round

Time based elimination. Complete the track in minimum time to qualify for the next round.

02

Qualifier Round

Minimum human intervention + time based. Test your robot's autonomous capabilities along with speed.

03

Final Round

Track revealed on spot. Completion + time based scoring. Adapt quickly and race to victory!

Robot Specifications

[ Requirements ]

Maximum dimensions: 30 × 30 cm (L × B)

Robot may be wired or wireless

Maximum weight: 5 kg

No readymade toy cars allowed

No pneumatic/hydraulic systems or IC engines

Learning Outcomes

[ What You'll Gain ]

Teamwork experience

Improved creativity & analytical skills

Robot ergonomics & aerodynamics

Drive systems (4WD, RWD, FWD)

Steering mechanism understanding
