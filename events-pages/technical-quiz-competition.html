A knowledge-based contest testing your expertise in technical and general awareness! Compete in multiple rounds covering science, technology, and current affairs. Put your knowledge to the test and prove you're the ultimate quiz champions!

Competition Rounds

[ 4 Rounds ]

01

Preliminary Round

25 MCQs in 25 minutes (1 mark each). Top 6 teams qualify for the finals.

02

Final Round 1: Science & Technology

Questions covering physics, chemistry, biology, engineering, and technological innovations.

03

Test your knowledge on recent events, news, and global happenings.

04

Final Round 3: Rapid Fire

Quick-fire questions where speed and accuracy determine the winner!
