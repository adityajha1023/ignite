"Scan, Solve, Seek - The Ultimate QR Adventure"

Team Size

An exciting QR-code based treasure hunt that tests your problem-solving skills and teamwork! Navigate through the campus, decode clues, and race against time to find the ultimate treasure. Are you ready to become the ultimate treasure hunters?

How It Works

[ Step by Step ]

01

Start at Booth 1

Scan the QR code to receive your first clue. The adventure begins!

02

Solve the Clue

Work with your team to decode the clue and photograph the solution.

03

Show & Scan

Present your photo at the next booth and scan for the next clue.

04

Reach the Destination

Repeat the process until you reach the final destination and claim victory!

Competition Rules

[ Guidelines ]

Team of 3-5 members

Mobile phones mandatory to scan QR codes

Visit all booths in the correct sequence

No external help or shortcuts allowed

Complete the hunt within the time limit

Fastest team to complete wins

What to Bring

[ Essentials ]

Smartphone with camera

QR code scanner app

Charged battery / power bank

Comfortable footwear

Team spirit & enthusiasm

Water bottle
