Welcome to our annual inter-school competition, where creativity meets intellect! This year's theme, "Concept Showcase," encourages students to present innovative ideas through model presentations. It's a platform to explore creativity, problem-solving, and presentation skills while addressing various subjects like science, technology, social issues, and sustainability.

Event Theme

[ Concept Showcase ]

The theme encourages students to think beyond boundaries and present their innovative solutions to real-world problems. Whether it's a scientific breakthrough, a sustainable living concept, or a technological advancement â€“ showcase your creativity and impress the judges!

Competition Rules

[ Guidelines ]

Team Composition: 2-4 students from the same school

Theme Adherence: Models must align with "Concept Showcase" and reflect innovation

Documentation: A detailed written document outlining the concept, significance, and impact is mandatory

Originality: Only original ideas; plagiarism leads to disqualification

Materials & Props: Teams bring their own materials; hazardous items are prohibited

Time Management: Each team gets a set time for presentation followed by Q&A

[ Evaluation ]
