Poster Presentation

"Visualize Your Research - Present Your Ideas"

Registration Fee / ₹200

Event Category

Tech Event

Team Size

Showcase your research or projects through visually compelling posters on technical topics. This event provides a platform for students to present their innovative ideas, research findings, and project work in a creative visual format that engages and informs the audience.

Competition Rules

[ Guidelines ]

Poster size: A1 (standard format)

Team of 2 members

Presentation time: 3-5 minutes

Topic must be technical and written at the top of the poster

Poster can use pencil, crayon, oil paint, photographs, printouts, or 3D objects

Original work only; plagiarism leads to disqualification

Poster Guidelines

[ What to Include ]

Clear and concise title

Visual diagrams and charts

Key findings or conclusions

Methodology overview

Team member names

References (if applicable)
